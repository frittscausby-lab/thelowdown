Live at Ted’s Proves Smaller is Better

Named for the original owners Ted and Julia Jewell, Live at Ted’s is located in a converted bungalow-style house under the Cape Fear Memorial Bridge. With a capacity of 70 people, it is Wilmington’s only listening room — and the case for small venues has never been stronger.
